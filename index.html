The Verandah Story

What started as a simple home has transformed into one of Vijayawada’s most loved café spaces. At Verandah, every corner feels familiar, every cup feels crafted, and every visit feels like time well spent.

Known for its elegant yet cozy ambience, Verandah blends premium coffee with comfort food — from rich pastas to indulgent desserts. It’s where people come not just to eat, but to slow down.
